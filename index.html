Keeping it near $50 a day

  • Restaurant mains run R$45–90; one nice dinner + lighter days stays on budget.
  • Museums are R$0–30; all the nature is free — your budget cushions.
  • Massages run R$150–250; packages can push past the daily cap.
  • Rodízio steakhouses are worth it but a splurge — plan around one.
  • Uber is cheap and covers every stop here; skip the rental car.
  • Prices are rough Jul 2026 estimates — confirm when booking.
